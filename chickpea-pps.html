Chickpea Salad Sandwich

A fun & easy Pita pocket chickpea salad sandwich recipe

Ingredients:

⁘ Whole Wheat Flax Oat Bran and Mini Pocket Pita Bread

⁘ Veggies: Ripe Avacado, Lettuce, Tomato, Onion

⁘ Salt Pepper Paprika Onion Powder Cayenne Cajun Harissa & Curry

⁘ 1 bag of Chickpeas. Chickpea water.

⁘ 1 Saucepan/Wok

Directions

Step 1: Pour & boil the chickpeas in a small pot, pinch of salt & boil until chickpeas are soft. Set aside 1-cup of the boiked chickpea water.

Step 2: In a hot saucepan, pour a tablespoon size amount of olive oil, add some minced garlic & onions, let mix for 20-30 sec.

Step 3: Pour the cooked chickpeas, adding a pinch of sea salt, a teaspoon of Pepper, Paprika, and Onion Powder. 1/2 teaspoon of Powder Cayenne, Cajun, Harissa & Curry.

Step 4: Clean & rinse the tomato & lettuce. Slice & dice the Avacado, Lettuce, Tomato, and Onions. Then set them aside on a flat plate.

Step 5: Mix & stir all seasonings added in step 4, & add 1/2-cup of chickpea water. let it cook on medium high heat for 10min. Cover the saucepan after 10min & leave on low heat until served.

Step 6: Clean & rinse the tomato & lettuce. Slice & dice the Lettuce, Tomato, and Onions. Then set them aside on a flat plate

Step 7: Cut open the top of a mini pocket pita bread, revealing a pocket.

Step 8: Place the veggies you set a side on the flat plate in any order & the chickpeas inside the pita pocket.

enjoy!

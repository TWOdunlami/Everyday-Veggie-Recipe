Starch & Veggie Plate

Dive into this mouth-watering bowl!

Ingredients:

⁘ Batata (White sweet potato)

⁘ Sweet Bell Pepper

⁘ Ripe Avacado

⁘ Chili Garlic Rice

⁘ Mushrooms

Directions

Step 1: Gently clean the white sweet potato, cut them into 4 pieces, and place them in a steamer-pot for 45 mins or until softened.

Step 2: Cut sweet bell peppers into vertical pieces.

Step 3: Cut and Thinly slice your ripe avacado

Step 4: Mushrooms should be cooked with light oil in a saucepan. Recommended seasoning: Salt, Pepper, Cayene, Paprika, Curry Garlic & Onion powder.

Chili Garlic Rice Recipe

Step 1: Mince and chop 4 garlic cloves, 3 green onion sticks, 6 dried chili peppers, and chili oil

Step 2: Place minced ingredients and chili oil in a lightly oiled saucepan.

Step 3: Stir occassionally for 3-5 minutes.

Step 4: Mix in the white rice(fresh or leftover) and soy sauce. Saute for 5-7 minutes. All set! 🍛

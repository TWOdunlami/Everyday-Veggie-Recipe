Bean Avacado Recipe

Enjoy Our Easy & fast Mouth-Watering Sandwich

Ingredients:

⁘ 2 Slices of Ezekial Bread

⁘ 1 Avacado(Ripe)

⁘ 1 Tomato

⁘ 1 Onion

⁘ Pre-Made Beans

Directions

Step 1: Place the 2 slices of bread in the toaster, set the heat level to 3.

Step 2: thinly slice the avacado, onions, and tomatoes.

Step 3: Check your leftovers and see if you have any pre-made beans, if so, set a side a small portion, place them in an air-fryer. for 8mins.

Step 4: Unload your toasted Ezekial bread.

Step 5: Use the left slice of bread to place the tomatoes, onions & avacado slices.

Step 6: Use the right slice of bread to place your left-over beans that are are finished warming up in the air fryer.

enjoy!

STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS

Protein & Veggie rich dish!

Ingredients:

⁘ 3 Green Peppers, 3 cups of Quinoa, 3 Avocado.

⁘ 1/2-lbs of mushrooms, 2 sweet potatoes.

⁘ 1 red onion, 2 tomatoes, minced garlic.

⁘ Pre-Made Beans(insert link)

Directions

Step 1: Quinoa, add minced onion, garlic, diced tomatoes & lime. stir until blended.

Step 2: Mushrooms, sautee in medium heat & season(salt, ground pepper, pepper flakes, garlic powder, cayenne, cajun powder) as desired.

Step 3: Sweet Peppers, cut the top open. Bake at 250-F for 15 min.

Step 4: Steam the sweet potatoes until its soft, then cut them into cubes. Cut the avocado into even slices.

Step 5: As desired add the Pre-made beans, Quinoa salad, Mushrooms, Sweet potatoes, Avocado

Step 6: Put stuffed eppers in the oven at 300-F for 20min. Then serve.

enjoy!

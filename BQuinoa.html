Bean Quinoa Recipe

Dive into this bowl of rich nutrients!

Ingredients:

⁘ 1 Avacado(Ripe)

⁘ Steamed Sweet Potato

⁘ Pre-Made Mushrooms

⁘ Pre-Made Quinoa

⁘ Pre-Made Beans

Directions

Step 1: Pre-Made Quinoa has raw cut onions & sweet peppers.

Step 2: Pre-Made Mushrooms should be lightly seasoned and cooked with light oil in a saucepan.

Step 3: Pre-Made beans of your choice.

Step 4: Gently clean the sweet potato, and place them in a steamer-pot. 45 mins for the sweet potato to soften.

Step 5: Thinly slice the avacado.

Step 6: Place all your ingredients in a nice bowl and enjoy every nutrious bite.

enjoy!

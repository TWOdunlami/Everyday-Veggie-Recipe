Veggie Medley Recipe

Enjoy our mix & match veggie medley

Ingredients:

⁘ Brussell Sprouts

⁘ 1 Avacado

⁘ Pre-made Quinoa

⁘ Sweet Potato

⁘ Pre-made Beans

Directions

Step 1: Cut and sautee your Brussel Sprouts in a saucepan. Add pepper, and smoked paprika for flavor.

Step 2: Cut and Thinly slice the avacado.

Step 3: Pre-Made Quinoa has raw cut onions & sweet peppers.

Step 4: Gently clean the sweet potato, and place them in a steamer-pot. 45 mins for the sweet potato to soften.

Step 5: Pre-Made beans of your choice.

enjoy!

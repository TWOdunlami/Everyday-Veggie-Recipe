Jollof and Mushrooms Recipe

Get a biteful everytime with our Jollof, Mushroom & Veggie combo!

Ingredients:

⁘ Sweet Peppers

⁘ Cucumber

⁘ Sweet Potato

⁘ Pre-Made Jollof Rice

⁘ Pre-Made mushrooms

Directions

Step 1: Gently clean the sweet potato, and place them in a steamer-pot. 45 mins for the sweet potato to soften.

Step 2: Check your refridgerator for pre-made Jollof and Mushrooms, and portion out your bowl.

Step 3: Gently clean the sweet peppers, and Cucumber, with lime and rinse with water.

Step 4: Once cleaned, cut them vertically and as thinly as you prefer.

Step 5: Place the sliced vegatbles in your bowl and enjoy the hearty combo!
